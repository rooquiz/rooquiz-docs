# RooQuiz Documentation

RooQuiz is a platform for creating, distributing, and analyzing **quizzes, assessments, and surveys**. Whether you are running a knowledge test, a personality/ability assessment, or a market research survey, you can build questions visually with drag-and-drop, distribute them via link or QR code, and review responses and analytics in real time.

<Callout type="info">
  This documentation is written for **creators** — users who build and manage quizzes in the dashboard. Documentation for respondents will be added later.
</Callout>

## What RooQuiz can do

- **Four quiz types**: Quiz, Scorecard, Outcome Quiz, and Random Quiz — covering scoring, multi-dimensional analysis, outcome typing, and randomized question pools.
- **Rich question types**: 14+ types including single choice, multiple choice, dropdown, cascade, rating, true/false, fill-in-the-blank, and ordering.
- **Conditional logic**: Show or hide later questions based on previous answers.
- **Scoring & AI evaluation**: Automatic scoring, multi-dimensional radar analysis, and AI evaluation of text answers (subject to quota).
- **Multiple distribution channels**: Public links, QR codes, social share cards (OG), and UTM source tracking.
- **Results & analytics**: Response management, data export, visual insights, and report generation.
- **Team collaboration**: Multi-member organizations, roles and permissions, custom domains, and more (advanced features, covered later).

## The four quiz types at a glance

| Type | Best for | Core capabilities |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Quiz** | Knowledge tests, online exams, scored practice | Fixed order, scoring, right/wrong judgment & feedback |
| **Scorecard** | Personality/ability assessments, surveys | Multi-dimensional analysis, conditional logic, AI reports |
| **Outcome Quiz** | Personality quizzes, "which X are you", diagnostic lead magnets | Choice voting, typed result pages with CTAs |
| **Random Quiz** | Practice drills, randomized testing | Pulls random questions from a question bank each time |

<Callout type="warning">
  The quiz type is chosen at creation time and **cannot be changed afterward**. Confirm your use case before you start. See [The Four Quiz Types](/en/getting-started/quiz-types).
</Callout>
