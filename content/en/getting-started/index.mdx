# Getting Started

This chapter walks you through the full journey — from signing up to publishing your first quiz — in just a few minutes.

## The overall flow

<Steps>
### Sign up and sign in

Create an account, verify your email, and enter the dashboard. See [Sign Up & Sign In](/en/getting-started/account).

### Get familiar with the dashboard

The dashboard is the hub for managing all your quizzes. See [Dashboard Tour](/en/getting-started/dashboard).

### Create your first quiz

Start from blank or from a template, and choose a quiz type. See [Create Your First Quiz](/en/getting-started/create-quiz).

### Edit your questions

Add questions, set logic and scoring in the visual editor. See [Editor & Question Types](/en/editor).

### Publish and share

Generate a public link or QR code and distribute it to respondents. See [Publish & Share](/en/publish).

### Review results

View and analyze responses in records and insights. See [Responses & Analytics](/en/results).
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Running through the main flow once before diving into advanced settings is the fastest way to get comfortable with RooQuiz.
</Callout>
