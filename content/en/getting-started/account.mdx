# Sign Up & Sign In

## Create an account

<Steps>
### Open the registration page

Visit the dashboard site and go to the **Register** page.

### Enter email and password

Enter an email you use regularly and set a password to register.

### Verify your email

After registering, the system sends a verification email. Click the link in it to verify your address. Full functionality is available only after verification.
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  If you were invited to a team, click the link in the invitation email directly. The system will guide you through signing up or signing in and automatically add you to that team.
</Callout>

## Sign in

On the **Login** page, enter your email and password. In some cases third-party sign-in is also supported.

## Reset your password

If you forget your password, click **Forgot password** on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the reset link the system sends to set a new password.

## Accounts and teams

- One account can belong to multiple **teams (organizations)**, and data is isolated between teams.
- Your role in each team (owner / member) determines what you can do.
- Switching teams, managing members, and similar actions happen in the dashboard settings (advanced — see the upcoming team documentation).

<Callout type="warning">
  Register with a real, reachable email address: it is used for verification, password recovery, notifications, and team invitations.
</Callout>
