# Scoring & Assessment Rules

"Scoring" means different things by quiz type: Quiz focuses on **right/wrong and totals**, while Scorecard focuses on **multi-dimensional analysis and reports**.

## Quiz: scoring and judgment

In a **Quiz** you can:

- **Set points**: Assign points to questions or correct options.
- **Judge right/wrong**: Mark correct answers; the system judges each question automatically.
- **Sum totals**: Points are added up automatically.
- **Passing score**: Set a pass threshold and show a pass/fail result.
- **Answer feedback**: Show respondents the correctness and explanation per question.

<Callout type="info">
  Scoring-related settings are mainly available in the Quiz type.
</Callout>

## Scorecard: dimensional analysis and AI evaluation

In a **Scorecard**, scoring is more about "profiling" than right/wrong:

- **Multi-dimensional scoring**: Map questions to several dimensions; after submission, each dimension gets a score, often shown as a **radar chart**.
- **Result profile**: Produce personality/ability conclusions based on dimension scores.
- **AI evaluation**: Use AI to evaluate text answers for deeper insight.

### About AI evaluation quota

<Callout type="warning">
  AI evaluation consumes your team's **AI quota**. Quota is counted per cycle; once exceeded, AI evaluation is skipped for that cycle (basic scoring is still generated). See [AI Credits & Billing](/en/account/ai-billing) for billing, monthly resets, and top-ups.
</Callout>

## Report generation

- **Synchronous**: Basic scoring is calculated immediately after submission.
- **Asynchronous**: Heavier computation such as dimensional analysis and AI evaluation is generated asynchronously; reports go through a "generating → completed" state, and the system retries automatically on failure.

For viewing, exporting, and delivering reports, see [Reports & AI Evaluation](/en/results/reports).
