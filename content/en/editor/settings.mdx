# Form Settings

Beyond questions, you can configure the appearance and global options of the whole quiz so it fits your brand and audience.

## Appearance & branding

- **Theme & brand color**: Set the primary color to unify the visual style.
- **Cover image**: Add a cover to make the quiz feel more polished and recognizable.
- **Flag color / marker**: Use colors or markers to distinguish quizzes in the list.

## Languages

- A quiz itself can offer **multiple language versions**, and respondents can switch language on the answer page (when the corresponding localization is enabled).
- This is different from the docs site's English/Chinese switch: the former is for **respondents**, the latter for **creators reading the docs**.

## Basic information

- **Name / title**: Used to identify the quiz in the dashboard and shown on the answer page.
- **Description**: A short summary of the quiz that can be shown to respondents.

## Sharing settings

Social share cards (OG), public links, and source tracking live in the share area — see [Publish & Share](/en/publish).
