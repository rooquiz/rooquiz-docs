# Reports & AI Evaluation

For the **Scorecard** type, each response can generate a **report** presenting dimension scores, profile conclusions, and (optionally) AI evaluation.

## What's in a report

- **Dimension scores**: Scores per dimension, often shown as a **radar chart**.
- **Profile conclusions**: Personality/ability conclusions derived from dimension scores.
- **AI evaluation**: AI analysis of text answers (subject to quota).

## How a report is generated

- **Basic scoring** is calculated **immediately** after submission.
- **Heavier computation** such as dimensional analysis and AI evaluation is generated **asynchronously**; the report goes through a "generating → completed" state.
- If generation fails, the system **retries automatically** within a certain window.

<Callout type="info">
  If a report stays in "generating" for a long time, refresh later; if the problem persists, try regenerating or contact support.
</Callout>

## Viewing and delivery

- **On-screen**: View directly in the dashboard; you can also let respondents view their own report via a share link.
- **PDF export**: Export the report to PDF for archiving or offline distribution.
- **Email delivery**: Send the report by email. **Email delivery is a Pro feature.**

## Result page CTA button

You can configure a call-to-action (CTA) button for **each score level**; respondents landing in that level see it at the bottom of their report — e.g. "Needs improvement" leads to booking a consultation, while "Excellent" leads to downloading advanced material.

- In **Editor** → **Report settings** → **Score levels**, toggle on the **CTA button** for a level and fill in the button text and link; the lead-in text and "open in new window" are optional.
- To use the same button across levels, configure one and click **Apply to all levels**.
- CTA edits **take effect immediately on all historical reports** — swap campaign links or copy without worrying about stale buttons in old reports.
- Respondents' CTA clicks feed into the conversion funnel in [Insights](/en/results/insights).

<Callout type="info">
  Links must start with `http(s)://`. The button style automatically follows your quiz theme color.
</Callout>

## About AI quota

<Callout type="warning">
  AI evaluation consumes your team's **AI quota**, which resets per cycle. When the quota is exceeded, AI evaluation is skipped for that cycle, but basic scoring and dimensional analysis are still generated normally. See [AI Credits & Billing](/en/account/ai-billing) for allowances, monthly reset rules, upgrades, and top-ups.
</Callout>
