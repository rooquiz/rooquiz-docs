# Responses & Analytics

After a quiz is published, all responses flow into the dashboard. Here you can view every response, export data, see visual analysis, and generate reports for assessments.

<Cards>

  <Cards.Card title="Reports & AI Evaluation" href="/en/results/reports" />
</Cards>

## How they differ

| Module | Focus | Best for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Response Records** | Details of individual responses | Reviewing, filtering, and exporting raw data |
| **Insights** | Aggregate stats and charts | Completion rate and overall distribution by question |
| **Reports** | Analysis of a single response | Assessment scores, dimensional profiles, and AI evaluation |

<Callout type="info">
  Use records for raw data, insights for overall trends, and reports for individual conclusions. Combining all three gives the fullest picture of your responses.
</Callout>
