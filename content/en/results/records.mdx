# Response Records

Response records show **every submission** a quiz has received — the place to view raw data.

## What you can do

- **View the list**: Browse all submissions in a table/list with submission time, source, and more.
- **Filter and search**: Narrow down by time, completion status, keyword, and other criteria.
- **View a single response**: Open any response to see the exact answer to each question.
- **Export data**: Export responses to CSV / Excel for further processing in external tools.

## Information in a record

Besides the answers, a record usually carries:

- **Serial number**: A response number that increments within each quiz.
- **Source info**: If submitted via a [UTM link](/en/publish/tracking), the corresponding source is recorded.
- **Submission metadata**: Such as submission time.

## Export tips

<Callout type="info">
  Before exporting, use filters to scope the data you need (e.g. a time range or a specific source) to keep the exported file focused and easy to analyze.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
  Exported data may contain personal information provided by respondents. Store and use it in accordance with your organization's privacy and compliance requirements.
</Callout>
