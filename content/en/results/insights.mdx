# Insights

Insights aggregate scattered responses into **stats and charts**, helping you grasp the big picture without scrolling through records one by one.

## Common metrics and charts

- **Completion rate**: The ratio of started to completed submissions, reflecting drop-off.
- **Submission volume**: Total responses and their trend over time.
- **Question-level distribution**: For choice questions, the share and distribution of each option.
- **Source distribution**: Combined with [UTM Source Tracking](/en/publish/tracking), compare response volume across channels.

## Suggestions

- Use **completion rate** to find where respondents tend to drop off, and optimize the number or order of questions.
- Use **option distribution** to understand respondents' overall tendencies.
- Combine with time trends to evaluate the effect of a campaign.

<Callout type="info">
  Insights show "group trends." To dig into a specific response, go to [Response Records](/en/results/records); for individual assessment conclusions, go to [Reports](/en/results/reports).
</Callout>
