# Insights

Insights aggregate scattered responses into **stats and charts**, helping you grasp the big picture without scrolling through records one by one.

## Common metrics and charts

- **Completion rate**: The ratio of started to completed submissions, reflecting drop-off.
- **Submission volume**: Total responses and their trend over time.
- **Question-level distribution**: For choice questions, the share and distribution of each option.
- **Source distribution**: Combined with [UTM Source Tracking](/en/publish/tracking), compare response volume across channels.

## Conversion funnel

The funnel tracks **one visit (session)** through five stages: **Viewed → Started → Submitted → Report viewed → CTA clicked**. How each stage is counted:

| Stage | Counted when |
| --- | --- |
| Viewed | The quiz page actually opens in a browser (crawlers and admin previews excluded) |
| Started | The respondent answers any question for the first time (not just clicking "start") |
| Submitted | The submission succeeds |
| Report viewed | The report finishes generating while the respondent is on the result page |
| CTA clicked | The respondent clicks the [CTA button](/en/results/reports#result-page-cta-button) at the bottom of the report |

The percentage next to each stage is the conversion rate **relative to the previous stage**, so you can see exactly where the drop happens.

### Top 10 drop-off points

Among sessions that **didn't submit** but answered at least one question, grouped by the **last question they answered** — questions with high counts are where people give up: too hard, too sensitive, or the next one scared them off. Visitors who left without answering anything aren't listed here; they show up in the Viewed → Started decline.

### Conversion by source

Grouped by the source tag on the link (`?from=`, generated by [Source Tracking](/en/publish/tracking) or [Embed](/en/publish/embed)), comparing viewed/started/submitted counts and completion rate per source; sources coming from website embeds carry an **Embed** tag.

<Callout type="info">
  The funnel counts sessions since the feature launched; earlier historical submissions are not backfilled, so "total submissions" may initially exceed the funnel's submitted count. They converge over time.
</Callout>

## Suggestions

- Use the **conversion funnel** to locate where the drop happens: many views but few starts points to the landing page or the first question; many starts but few submissions — check the top drop-off questions.
- Use **option distribution** to understand respondents' overall tendencies.
- Combine time trends with **conversion by source** to evaluate each channel's campaigns.

<Callout type="info">
  Insights show "group trends." To dig into a specific response, go to [Response Records](/en/results/records); for individual assessment conclusions, go to [Reports](/en/results/reports).
</Callout>
