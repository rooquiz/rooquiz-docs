# Social Share Card (OG)

When you share a quiz link to platforms like WeChat, X/Weibo, or Slack, the platform automatically fetches the **social share card** (Open Graph info) to generate a preview. Customizing this card noticeably improves click-through.

## What you can customize

- **Title**: The main heading shown on the card.
- **Description**: A short note below the title.
- **Preview image**: The card's image — use something clear and appealing.

## How to set it

In the target quiz, open **Share** → **Social card / OG**, fill in the title and description, upload a preview image, and save.

## Tips

- Keep the title concise and compelling, conveying the quiz's topic and value.
- Add a call to action in the description (e.g. "Find out your … in 3 minutes").
- Make the preview image's aspect ratio and clarity suitable for major platforms.

<Callout type="info">
  Some platforms **cache** the fetched card. If the preview doesn't change after an update, it's usually caching — try again later or refresh with the platform's debugging tool.
</Callout>
