# Publish & Share

Once your quiz is built, distribute it to respondents from the share area. RooQuiz offers links, QR codes, social cards, and source tracking.

## Pre-publish checklist

- Questions and logic have been verified with **preview**.
- Scoring / assessment rules are configured correctly (if needed).
- Appearance, cover, and basic info are set.
- The quiz is in an **active / answerable** state.

<Callout type="warning">
  If respondents open the link but cannot answer, confirm the quiz is active and has not been disabled or restricted.
</Callout>
