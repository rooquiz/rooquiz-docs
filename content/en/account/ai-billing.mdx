# AI Credits & Billing

AI features in your quizzes (AI evaluation, AI auto-grading, etc.) are billed by **actual usage** and consume your team's **AI credits**. This page explains what consumes credits, how billing works, when credits reset, and what happens when you run out.

## What consumes AI credits

- **Assessment AI evaluation**: AI analysis of text answers that produces richer report insights.
- **AI suggestions**: improvement/interpretation suggestions generated by AI in the report.
- **Fill-in-the-blank AI auto-grading** in Quiz / Random Quiz: when a fill-in-the-blank question enables "AI auto-grading", AI decides whether the answer is correct.

Regular choice-question scoring, dimensional scoring, and template-generated reports **do not** consume AI credits.

## Billing unit & models

- AI credits are measured in **Neurons** (Cloudflare Workers AI's billing unit).
- Each AI call is charged by the number of **tokens** consumed, converted to Neurons: more input/output tokens means more consumption.
- You can pick an AI model tier under **Settings → Plan**: **Standard / Advanced / Flagship**. Stronger models consume more Neurons per call. Free teams can use "Standard" only; Advanced and Flagship are **Pro-only**.
- Charges are **settled asynchronously after** the report is generated, so they never slow down report generation.

<Callout type="info">
  Credits are only deducted when AI **actually runs successfully**; empty evaluations or failed calls are not charged.
</Callout>

## Monthly allowance & reset (billing anniversary)

- Every team gets a monthly AI allowance whose size depends on the plan: Free has a base allowance, Pro has a larger one. See the exact figures under **Settings → Plan**.
- The allowance **resets monthly**, on the **billing anniversary** (not a fixed 30 days):
  - **Pro**: follows the subscription billing date; a new cycle starts on each renewal.
  - **Free**: resets monthly on the team's own cycle.
- Unused monthly allowance **does not roll over**; it is cleared at cycle end and reissued next cycle.
- The current cycle's reset date is shown under **Settings → Plan → Cycle reset**.

## Topping up (Pro)

- Pro teams can buy extra AI credits under **Settings → Plan**.
- Pricing: **1 unit = 30,000 Neurons = $1**, with a minimum of 5 units and a maximum of 100 units per purchase.
- Top-up balance **never expires** and is not cleared by the monthly cycle.
- Consumption order: **the monthly allowance is used first, then the top-up balance**.

## What happens when credits run out

The behavior differs by how AI is used:

| Scenario | Behavior when credits are exhausted |
| --- | --- |
| **Assessment AI evaluation / AI suggestions** | **Graceful degradation**: the AI parts are skipped, while the report, dimensional analysis, and basic scoring are **still generated normally** |
| **Fill-in-the-blank AI auto-grading** in Quiz / Random Quiz | New submissions for those forms are **rejected** (AI credits exhausted), because grading depends on AI and cannot be computed offline |

<Callout type="warning">
  If you are collecting responses for a Quiz that uses "AI auto-grading", keep an eye on your balance — running out will prevent respondents from **submitting**. Restore it by upgrading to Pro, topping up, or waiting for the next cycle reset.
</Callout>

## Low-balance reminder

- When a team's **total available credits** (remaining monthly allowance + top-up balance) drop **below 20%** of the plan's monthly allowance, the system checks daily and sends a reminder email to the team **owner**.
- Only **one reminder per cycle**; it re-arms after the allowance resets or a successful top-up.

## Where to view and manage

Go to **Settings → Plan** to see: current cycle allowance and remaining, top-up balance, cycle reset date, AI model tier selection, and the top-up entry.
